Overview

Objective: CSAT although a qualifying paper have become a major roadblock for the students in clearing Prelims Exam. Therefore, SUNYA IAS CSAT Foundation Course is designed to provide the CONCEPTUAL UNDERSTANDING of topics making it easier for each student (especially non-Maths background) to score 100+ marks in CSAT.

WHY this COURSE?

  • FOCUS upon CONCEPTS: We do not believe in formulas or tricks. We strongly believe that basics and conceptual clarity is the only way to clear this Exam.
  • FOCUS upon BASICS: We duly focus upon basics so as to make you comfortable with CSAT from Day 1.
  • FOCUS upon APTITUDE: We believe that UPSC is testing aptitude through this exam. Hence, our classes also focus upon aptitude building along with calculations.
  • MENTORSHIP Support: by faculties themselves.
